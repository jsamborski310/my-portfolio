About

I am a UX/ UI designer and WordPress developer currently residing in Washington. I am forever curious. Interested in the interesting. Perpetually looking for ways to create designs that are bold, clean, and functional, and websites that are intuitive, simple and convenient.

Over the past five years, I’ve partnered with incredible non-profit organizations, medical associations, and small businesses to design and develop WordPress websites that streamlines their process, provides them with functionality that enhances their operations, and is useful and helpful to their users.

Currently, I’m working on the UI for a software application that will make legal more accessible and affordable, and leveling up my skills to include full stack web development. I also run a book publishing company I established in 2011 because I am fiercely passionate about books.
